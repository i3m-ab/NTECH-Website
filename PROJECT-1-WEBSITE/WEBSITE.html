N-TECH Training Centre

N-TECH Solutions is an ICT repair and maintenance training organization for women by women.

Founded in 2020, N-TECH Solutions has been providing a safe space and maintain privacy of digital content for ladies especially those who are not culturally comfortable to have their digital devices repaired by male technicians, and to enlighten our clients on improving their businesses and lifestyle by adopting a secured technological way of life.
